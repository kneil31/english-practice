# Vocabulary

Words worth remembering from sessions and reading.

- **slippery slope** — a bad direction that leads to worse outcomes
  - *Ignoring small debts could be a slippery slope.*

- **ecstatic** — extremely happy or excited
  - *I was ecstatic when I got my first photography booking.*

- **psychological safety** — feeling safe to speak honestly in a group
  - *Teams need psychological safety to share ideas without fear.*

- **collaboration** — working together toward a shared goal
  - *Good collaboration between teams leads to better results.*

- **dedication** — commitment to a task or purpose
  - *Dedication helps you stay consistent even when it's hard.*
